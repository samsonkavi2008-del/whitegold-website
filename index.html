Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to bring? +
You need a valid government-issued photo ID such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, or Passport. This is required as per RBI guidelines.
How is the gold rate calculated? +
We use live MCX market rates updated every 30 seconds. The final price is weight × purity factor × current MCX rate per gram — shown transparently.
How long does the whole process take? +
Typically 10–20 minutes from walk-in to payment. XRF purity test takes 2 minutes, weighing 1 minute, and payment is instant.
Do you buy broken or damaged jewelry? +
Yes! We buy all types of gold jewelry regardless of condition — broken, bent, or damaged. We evaluate purely based on gold content.
What payment methods do you offer? +
Instant cash, UPI transfer, NEFT/RTGS bank transfer, and cheque. For amounts above ₹2 lakhs, digital payment is required per IT regulations.
Is there any minimum gold quantity? +
No minimum quantity. We buy even a single small earring at fair market rate.
Do you buy gold coins and bars? +
Yes, we buy gold coins, biscuits, and bars from any manufacturer including bank-issued coins and imported bars.
