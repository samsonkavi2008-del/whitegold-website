Frequently Asked
Everything you need to know
before you visit.
What types of gold jewelry do you buy?+
We buy all types — rings, necklaces, bangles, chains, earrings, anklets, and even broken or damaged pieces. We accept 14K, 18K, 22K, and 24K gold. No piece is too small or too large.
How is the price calculated?+
The price is: Net gold weight × Today's live MCX rate × Purity factor. This calculation is shown to you on screen before you accept anything. You can verify the rate on MCX's own website or any gold rate app.
How quickly will I receive payment?+
Payment is instant. Once you accept the offer, you receive cash, UPI transfer, or bank transfer on the spot — typically within 5 minutes. A digital receipt is sent to your phone immediately after.
What documents do I need to bring?+
You need one valid government-issued ID — Aadhaar card, PAN card, passport, or driving licence. For transactions above ₹50,000, PAN card is mandatory as per RBI guidelines.
Can I refuse the offer after evaluation?+
Absolutely. You are under no obligation to accept the offer. If you decline, your jewelry is returned to you immediately, free of charge, with no questions asked.
Is the weighing done in front of me?+
Yes, always. Your jewelry is never taken to a back room. All weighing and purity testing is done at the counter, in front of you. The scale display faces you so you can read it directly at all times.
Do you also buy gold coins and bars?+
Yes. We buy gold coins, bars, and bullion in addition to jewelry. The same transparent process applies — certified weight, live rate, instant payment.
