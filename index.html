Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before visiting a White Gold branch.

What documents do I need to bring?+
You need a valid government-issued photo ID — Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, or Passport. This is mandatory as per RBI guidelines for all gold purchase transactions above ₹20,000.
How is the gold rate calculated?+
We use live MCX market rates updated every 30 seconds. The final price is: Weight (grams) × Purity Factor × Current MCX Rate per gram. Everything is displayed transparently on our in-branch screen before you decide.
How long does the whole process take?+
Typically 15–20 minutes from walk-in to payment. The XRF purity test takes about 2 minutes, weighing takes 1 minute, and payment processing is instant via cash or UPI.
Do you buy broken or damaged jewelry?+
Absolutely yes. We buy all gold jewelry regardless of condition — broken, bent, scratched, or melted pieces. We evaluate purely based on gold content (weight × purity) and not appearance or design.
What payment methods are available?+
We offer instant cash, UPI transfer, NEFT/RTGS bank transfer, and account payee cheque. For amounts above ₹2 lakhs, digital payment is required as per Income Tax regulations.
Is there a minimum gold quantity requirement?+
No minimum quantity at all. We buy even a single small earring or the tiniest broken piece. Every gram of gold is valuable to us.
Do you buy gold coins and bars?+
Yes, we buy gold coins, biscuits, and bars from any manufacturer — including bank-issued coins (SBI, HDFC, etc.), temple coins, BIS hallmarked coins, and imported bars.
Is my transaction completely confidential?+
Yes. All transactions are 100% confidential. Your personal and financial details are never shared with any third party. We maintain full compliance with data privacy regulations.
