Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to bring? +
You need a valid government-issued photo ID such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, or Passport. This is required as per RBI guidelines for all gold purchase transactions.
How is the gold rate calculated? +
We use live MCX market rates updated every 30 seconds. The final price is weight × purity factor × current MCX rate per gram. Everything is shown transparently on our calculator.
How long does the whole process take? +
Typically 10–20 minutes from walk-in to payment. The XRF purity test takes 2 minutes, weighing 1 minute, and payment is instant via UPI or cash.
Do you buy broken or damaged jewelry? +
Yes! We buy all types of gold jewelry regardless of condition — broken, bent, or damaged. We evaluate purely based on gold content.
What payment methods do you offer? +
We offer instant cash, UPI transfer, NEFT/RTGS bank transfer, and cheque payment. For amounts above ₹2 lakhs, digital payment is required as per IT regulations.
Is there any minimum gold quantity? +
No minimum quantity. We buy even a single small earring. All gold is evaluated and bought at fair market rate.
Do you buy gold coins and bars? +
Yes, we buy gold coins, biscuits, and bars from any manufacturer including bank-issued coins, temple coins, and imported bars.
